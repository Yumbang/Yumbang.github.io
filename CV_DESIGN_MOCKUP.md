## Design Philosophy

### Principles Applied

1. **Clarity**: Information is easy to find and read
2. **Hierarchy**: Clear visual distinction between levels
3. **Consistency**: Repeated patterns for similar content
4. **Professionalism**: Corporate-appropriate aesthetic
5. **Accessibility**: Usable by everyone
6. **Performance**: Fast loading and rendering

### Design Decisions

**Why cards?**
- Clearly group related information
- Provide visual separation
- Enable hover effects for interactivity
- Scannable at a glance

**Why blue?**
- Professional color
- Good contrast with white
- Trustworthy and stable
- Works in print

**Why emojis for icons?**
- No image loading required
- Unicode support is universal
- Accessible (text-based)
- Can be replaced with SVG if preferred

**Why download button at top?**
- Immediate call-to-action
- Users often want PDF directly
- Prominent but not intrusive
- Web content supplements PDF
